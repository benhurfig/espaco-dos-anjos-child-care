Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 18, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you contact Espaço dos Anjos Child Care through our website, schedule a tour, request information, or communicate with us, we may collect personal information such as your name, phone number, email address, and any information you voluntarily provide regarding your childcare needs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to your inquiries, schedule daycare tours, communicate with families, provide information about our childcare programs, improve our services, and maintain a safe and efficient communication process.

3. Information Sharing

We respect your privacy and do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your information may only be shared when required by law or with trusted service providers who help us operate our website or communicate with families.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or misuse.

5. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with our website, improve performance, and provide a better browsing experience. These technologies do not collect sensitive personal information without your consent.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us directly. We will respond to your request as required by applicable laws.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page along with the revised effective date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us.
